evanescence

whispering within, wind
rain pattering on the sill
the quiet stiffness of the scent,
buried then carried away.
a methodical whimsy
carefully curated,
so my love may rest within your palm.
cradled gently, so it may not tear
and packaged neatly, so raindrops aren't mistaken
for a tear.
and out the sill it flows,
nature's tide whisking my quells away
